Welcome to My Research Portfolio

Hello! I'm Mahima Das, a psychological researcher specializing in the study of childhood trauma and its long-term impacts on emotional and behavioral outcomes. Currently pursuing my Masters in Research in Psychological Methods at the University of Sussex.

My work employs quantitative methods to understand complex psychological phenomena, with a focus on developing trauma-informed interventions for marginalized populations. I'm passionate about making psychological research accessible and applicable to real-world challenges.
